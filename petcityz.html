Project Overview

Petcityz is an Ecommerce platform for pets. The goals for Petcityz on social media were a little challenging. We have started from Zero audience to establish this brand on social media. Petcityz wanted to build their target audience, aware them with their products and services and they need more people to purchase from their website through social media platforms. We focus initially on the best content suitable for their audience and keep them engaging.

Facebook Ads

As per Petcityz business goals we are continuously working on to fulfill their small targets wrt. Website visits, Mobile app downloads, Lead generation for their services, Getting queries on messages and calls. Everything has been done effectively with our latest marketing data and skills. We believe in performance marketing on social media and constantly achieving beyond expectations.

Video Ad

Video content marketing is crucial for brands nowadays. It’s convenient for people to watch a video and also for brands to give their offerings effectively. We made video ads that really helped Petcityz to increase engagement, educating their audience, and allowing them to make the purchase decision.
